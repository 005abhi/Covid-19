Precautionary Methods:
  • Wear a mask: Ensure the mask covers your nose and mouth properly.
  • Maintain social distancing: Keep at least 6 feet (2 meters) away from others.
  • Wash hands frequently: Use soap and water for at least 20 seconds, or use a hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol.
  • Avoid touching your face: Especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Practice respiratory hygiene: Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your elbow when coughing or sneezing.
  • Stay home if unwell: Avoid going out if you experience symptoms like fever, cough, or difficulty breathing.
  • Get vaccinated: Follow local guidelines for COVID-19 vaccination.
